About

St Andrews Engineering Group (SEG) is a student-run society located in the University of St Andrews, dedicated to allowing students to develop engineering skills and providing a medium for like-minded individuals to share ideas, build electronic gadgets and have fun in the process.

People from all kind of backgrounds are welcome to join us. We expect no prior engineering knowledge and are keen to provide tools and lessons required to develop the necessary skills.

Every year we undertake a society-wide engineering project requiring the vast skillset and knowledge of all our members to complete.

Throughout each year we run workshops to teach and discuss various engineering topics, ranging from Electronics to Mechanics and Computer Science.

We want to utilize our scientific and technical knowledge in an engineering environment to allow us to learn, build and teach various aspects of engineering

Seg Manifesto

As a society we have committed to carry out the following pledges

Provide students with technical knowledge to work in an engineering field

Strengthen relations between our university and other programs/universities

Reach out and work with the local community in St Andrews

Bring together students and staff from multiple years and disciplines

Engage students in more extracurricular activities

Continue after all current members have graduates

F.I.A.S.C.O was our society weather balloon project for 2016/17, aiming to monitor and capture images of the stratosphere
